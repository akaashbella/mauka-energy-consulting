Mauka Energy

Consulting Project

Commercialization Strategy for Advanced Routing & Regenerative Braking Tools

Project Overview

A comprehensive commercialization strategy for Mauka Energy's innovative and patented routing tools designed to optimize heavy-duty electric vehicle performance through regenerative braking and energy-aware routing.

Project Objectives

Developed strategies for market entry, pricing models, and distribution channels to maximize impact in the EV fleet management space.

Customer Insights

Identified key value propositions through stakeholder interviews and user analysis, focusing on fleet electrification planning.

Market Opportunity

Identified a $2.1B TAM with initial SOM of $17M, focusing on heavy-duty EV fleets, mining equipment, and waste collection.

Key Findings

Competitive Advantage

Unique patented algorithm for modeling downhill energy gains, purpose-built for heavy-duty vehicles in rugged terrain.

Market Strategy

Recommended focus on stop-dense operations: port logistics, utility services, airport ground support, and municipal services.

Business Model

Proposed tiered SaaS offering with cloud-based tools, API access, and OEM integration partnerships.

Growth Potential

Projected Year 5 revenues exceeding $1.6M with $420K in profits, based on conservative adoption estimates.
